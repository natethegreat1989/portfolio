About Me

Hello Friend! Thanks for viewing my portfolio - it's no coincidence you're here. Here's a bit about me and what I've been working on.

I'm a 30 year old self taught software developer and entreprenuer. I'm passionate about thoughtfully using technology to solve real problems that we all run into as humans. From my diverse background I have a unique ear tuned for the little voice that says "wouldn't it be great if..." and I'm compelled to quickly go test those solutions. For most of my adult life I worked outside of the technology industry - oftentimes using software to supercharge my capabilities as a worker. After being unable to bring those ideas to life for so long, I jumped head first into learning software development!

Today you could find me working on developing software for businesses and artists in Wynwood. Longboarding with sk8pak on SoBe. I am at my best when my routine is "squared away" and includes a lot of excersize, journaling, and time blocked out to strategicly hit goals. Like an engine I like to fire on all cyclendars - and strategiclly do so.
